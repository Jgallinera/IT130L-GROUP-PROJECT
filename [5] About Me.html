About Me

Eman San Andres is the 19-year-old artist who created the kalsada heroes’ series, a collection of digital art focused on Filipino vendors who are part of the Filipino Culture. After graduating in his alumni- Pateros Catholic School, he has invested his time into opening up a page for his small business. He began accepting and doing commissions a year prior his’ Kalsada heroes’ debut. Now a sophomore in the Far Eastern University studying multi-media arts, he still continues to bring new characters into life and at the same time, reimagining different characters from different art style and integrating it to his own.
